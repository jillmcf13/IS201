Education

How college has helped me grow.

I came to college as a majoring in public health with an emphasis in health sciences, on the pre-med track, minoring in Spanish.

College has taught me how to focus on my passions rather than what I think others want me to do. In using public health as a "stepping stone", I've learned to love this field and grown to want a career in it.

My path has changed so much from when I first entered college but in the best way possible.
